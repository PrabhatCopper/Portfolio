VERIZON DASHBOARD KPI APP

TheNeed

Verizon, as one of the largest corporations on the planet, produces mountains of internal data every day.
If leveraged correctly, that data can give Verizon executives actionable intelligence to help them better do their jobs.
Unfortunately, the C-Suite didn’t have mobile access to timely and usable data.
They struggled to obtain key performance indicators for each business unit, and they could only access these KPIs via desktop or laptop computers.
The user interface on this system was neither intuitive nor engaging and Verizon did not have the available internal development resources to tackle this specific initiative.
So, Verizon partnered with Copper Mobile to solve this problem.

TheChallenge

  • Verizon utilizes multiple back-end services to collect,

    parse and analyze critical business data

  • Copper Mobile had to build a user interface from the ground up that

    could incorporate and filter results from all these different systems

  • The executive staff wanted real-time data, meaning the solution had to pull,

    analyze and display data from all these back end systems continuously

  • Most of the C-Suite were not mobile app development experts,

    meaning the solution had to be both intuitive and engaging



TheSolution

The proposed solution enabled the customer to achieve the business objectives by the following approach

  • Verizon utilizes multiple back-end services

    Verizon’s executives already had iPads and used iOS fluently, so it made the most sense to develop the Dashboard KPI for iPad usage. This solved the mobility problem and helped with the usability issue as well
